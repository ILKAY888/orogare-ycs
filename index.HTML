About YCS at Orogare

👥

What is YCS?

Young Catholic Students is an international movement in over 80 countries. At Orogare TTC, we bring students together to grow in faith, build friendships, and serve the community.

👁️

See • Judge • Act

Our methodology guides us to See realities, Judge them through the Gospel, and Act for positive transformation in our lives and community.

❤️

Why Join?

Balance technical training with spiritual growth. Develop leadership skills, serve others, and form lifelong friendships in a supportive Catholic community.
